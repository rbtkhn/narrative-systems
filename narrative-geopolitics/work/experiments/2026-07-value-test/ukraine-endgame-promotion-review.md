# Watch Promotion Review: Ukraine Battlefield and Negotiation Endgame

As of: `2026-07-09`

Decision: `remain-watch-candidate-after-comparison`

Candidate object:

> Whether Ukraine's battlefield deterioration and deep-strike strategy are
> closing negotiation room faster than external actors can reopen it.

## Promotion Test

| Gate | Result | Evidence |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Bounded crisis object | `pass` | The object is the relationship between battlefield deterioration, deep strikes, and diplomatic room—not the entire war. |
| Assertions, convergence, contested claims, and unknowns separated | `partial` | The comparison separates analyst assertions, overlapping convergence, and unknowns, but operational control, losses, and negotiating positions remain insufficiently adjudicated. |
| Source overlap and channel conditioning explicit | `pass` | Mercouris supplies sequence, Davis-hosted sources emphasize feasibility, and Mearsheimer-hosted appearances emphasize structure; repeated agreement inside these lanes is not independent confirmation. |
| Continuity changes judgment, confidence, contradiction handling, or falsifiability | `pass` | The controlled comparison changed the object from generalized battlefield pressure to the unobserved conversion between battlefield direction and implementable settlement terms. It also exposed source-lane overlap and rejected an elastic retrospective hook. |
| Observable review condition exists | `pass` | A documented change in territorial control, official negotiating terms, deep-strike authority, or alliance force commitments would be reviewable. |

Promotion requires every gate to pass. Result: `4 pass`, `1 partial`.

## Missing Evidence

- operational evidence sufficient to distinguish actual battlefield change from
  commentator repetition;
- explicit comparison of Russian, Ukrainian, and alliance negotiating terms;
- dated official settlement terms paired with an adjudicated operational
  threshold, so the competing conversion and hardening hypotheses can resolve.

The final falsifier now has a defined shape—movement in dated official terms
after an adjudicated battlefield or deep-strike threshold—but the archive does
not yet contain the baseline needed to run it.

The minimum qualifying evidence is now specified in the
[official-terms activation baseline](ukraine-official-terms-baseline.md). Current
coverage is `0 / 3` qualifying actor snapshots and `0 / 1` threshold record.

## Promotion Trigger

Re-run this review when a documented battlefield or policy threshold changes
and the system can compare at least two competing explanations of its effect on
negotiating room.

The [controlled comparison](ukraine-comparison-scorecard.md) found that
continuity materially improved three dimensions while both candidates passed
six of seven. That establishes method value but not promotion: the remaining
evidence gate is substantive, not architectural. No new probability-bearing
forecast is authorized now.
