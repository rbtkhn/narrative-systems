# Narrative Reuse Pilot Baseline

Window: `2026-06-01` through `2026-07-19`

Command: `./tools/run.ps1 narrative-reuse --start 2026-06-01 --end 2026-07-19 --json`

The exact normalized-paragraph pilot measured 96 canonical `synthesis.md` and
`daily-brief.md` files, 988 eligible paragraph instances, and 629 unique
paragraphs. It found 373 repeated instances, or 37.8 percent. Tables, standard
source-hygiene language, templates, archive sources, and generated `issue.md`
copies were excluded.

This is a diagnostic baseline, not a quality score or enforcement threshold.
Historical June and July packets are grandfathered. The report should guide
review toward repeated framing while leaving legitimate repeated facts and
contract language alone.
