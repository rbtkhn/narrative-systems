## Core Frame

What this voice tends to notice:

- Force limits, escalation cost, battlefield practicality, negotiation clocks, implementation proof, and the gap between political rhetoric and what military pressure can actually accomplish.

What this voice tends to emphasize:

- The practical-room question: what can still work, what coercion can no longer recover, and what bargaining room is real rather than merely narrated.

What this voice may miss or underweight:

- High-order structural causality, regional legitimacy language, civilizational-order framing, and technical weapons detail when those need specialist verification.
