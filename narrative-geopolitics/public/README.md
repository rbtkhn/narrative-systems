# Narrative Geopolitics Public Surface

This folder holds public-facing Narrative Geopolitics outputs.

The first public surfaces are source-bounded crisis briefs and intentionally
promoted daily briefs:

```text
public/briefs/daily/YYYY-MM-DD.md
```

Current crisis brief:

- [The Burden of Normalizing Hormuz](briefs/hormuz-transit-governance-2026-07-09.md)

Public briefs should be source-heavy, quote-aware, calm, and bounded. They may use short direct quotes when the wording matters.

`source-bounded` means the brief accurately represents and differentiates the
authorized archive but does not claim independent verification of operating
facts that the archive cannot establish.
