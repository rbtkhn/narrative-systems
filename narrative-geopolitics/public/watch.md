Recurring source attention alone is insufficient for promotion.

## Promotion Standard

A watch candidate becomes an active dossier only when all are true:

1. The crisis object is bounded enough to state in one sentence.
2. The archive distinguishes assertions, convergence, contested claims, and
   unknowns.
3. Source overlap and channel conditioning are explicit.
4. Continuity changes judgment, confidence, contradiction handling, or
   falsifiability.
5. At least one observable review condition exists; a new forecast is optional.

An active dossier returns to watch status when new intake no longer changes its
judgment, its review hooks become unresolvable, or it is sustained mainly by
repeated commentary rather than changed evidence.

## State Vocabulary

- `active-dossier`: bounded judgment under active review.
- `watch-candidate`: recurring signal awaiting a qualifying change.
- `watch-candidate-linked`: subordinate to another active crisis object.
- `watch-candidate-emerging`: potentially consequential but evidentially thin.
- `parked`: no qualifying change; retain continuity without active synthesis.
