## Queue Notes

CMC may contain useful search leads for every case, but none has been admitted
by this audit. Later dossiers must record candidates at a commit-pinned path and
independently resolve each extracted claim. A broad source list is not a claim
location; a quotation without a verified edition and location is excluded.

The audit intentionally leaves three lectures on hold. That is a result, not a
gap to conceal: the volume architecture can remain stable while drafting order
responds to the evidence.
